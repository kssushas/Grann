    About me

    Anna Kostroma

    Founder of Grann Pastry

    From hobby to own business.

    I graduated from DGF International Culinary School in Kyiv, and later studied at one of the world's most renowned hotel service and culinary schools, "Le Cordon Bleu" (France). I started my business after gaining experience and working in one of the largest pastry workshops in Georgia (1500 sq.m.).

    Currently, I have launched my own brand and am building my own pastry workshop. My work inspires me. Thanks to my pastries, I can create happy moments for you!
